Usage & Moderation Policy

To maintain a secure, productive, and respectful environment on the Aces's Lair platform, all users are required to adhere to the following policies. Violations are subject to moderation, including content removal and account suspension (ban), at the discretion of the administration.

Tier 1: Immediate & Permanent Ban

These are zero-tolerance offenses that compromise the integrity and security of the platform or its users.

  • Malicious Code Execution: Attempting to inject or run malicious scripts (e.g., XSS attacks) through any input field, or creating projects designed to steal data, credentials, or harm other users' systems.
  • Security Breaches & Hacking Attempts: Attempting to access another user's workspace, projects, or data without authorization. This includes exploiting vulnerabilities, brute-forcing credentials, or any other unauthorized access method.
  • Illegal Activities: Hosting, distributing, or linking to illegal content, including but not limited to pirated software, copyrighted material without permission, or content that violates local/international laws.
  • Harassment & Direct Threats: Issuing direct threats of violence or harm against another user or engaging in severe, targeted harassment.

Tier 2: Warning, then Ban

These offenses disrupt the community and user experience. A first-time offense may warrant a warning, but repeat behavior will result in a ban.

  • Spam & Phishing: Creating multiple low-quality projects for spamming purposes, or using any part of the platform to link to phishing sites or scams.
  • Impersonation: Creating an account with a name or email that intentionally impersonates another person to deceive others.
  • Abuse of Platform Resources: Intentionally creating projects or running code designed to degrade server performance or disrupt the service for other users.
  • Hate Speech & Discriminatory Content: Posting content that is racist, sexist, homophobic, or otherwise discriminatory against a protected group.

Tier 3: Discretionary / Minor Offenses

These are less severe but still negative behaviors. An admin may choose to delete the offending content or issue a warning. A ban is reserved for repeat offenders.

  • Inappropriate Content (Not Illegal): Using profile information, project names, or descriptions for content that is not safe for a professional work environment.
  • Circumventing Minor Rules: Exploiting minor loopholes in the site's functionality that do not harm other users or the platform's integrity.
